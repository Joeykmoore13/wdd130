Advanced techniques

This section is for full CFOP. This section builds a lot off of the intermediate section so it is definitly recomended to learn those methods first before doing very much with this section.

There are a lot of algorithms here and they will take a while to learn. Included in each section is a recomended order to memorize the algorithms based on difficulty and usefulness. Take your time though and don't try to memorize too much at once.
